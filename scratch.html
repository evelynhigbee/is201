My Love for Chopin

Jump to My Favorite Pieces

Growing up, I took piano lessons for 11 years. In that time, I played songs from a variety of composers, but something about Chopin always caught my attention. I've fallen in love with his soulful, emotional music that is so good at capturing human emotion, especially sorrow and triumph. I began to focus more on playing songs that he has written, and to this day his music is a go-to for me.
